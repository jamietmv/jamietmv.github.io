D-Bee

UI/UX

D-Bee was a cross-platform note taking app developed by Delta Research Center. It had enterprise features including create teams, share permission, domain specific markers. The goal of the project was to enable Delta employees to share content securely and organize notes effectively. My role in this project was to design both desktop and mobile version and to ensure a smooth user flow.

Cozy Date

Branding, Marketing

Cozy Date is a dating app that focuses on respectful in-person dating. In the app, users are able to see the other person's profile and availability without speculation. For this project, I developed Cozy Date's visual identity conveying genuineness and openness to reach young audiences who look for honest relationships.

My name is Jamie Chen. I am a senior visual & UI designer from Taiwan. I'm currently looking for new opportunities after working at VitalTek on Readict, a novel reading app with 1.2M+ users worldwide.

Prior to VitalTek, I worked at Delta Electronics Taipei Headquater on UI design for enterprise software and manufacturing systems. I also had experiences working at design agencies on a wide variety of projects from eCommerce UI design to prints, packaging, and branding. Project clients included Beats, Lyft, 49ers, UCSF, and Oakland Athletics.

I received my MFA degree in Graphic Design at Academy of Art University and BFA degree in Industrial Design. My goal is to create designs that's not only aesthetically pleasing but functional and logical. With my hard work, I never stop learning to become a better designer everyday.
